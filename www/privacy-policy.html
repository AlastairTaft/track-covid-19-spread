Privacy Policy - TAS Trace Together

We collect your location history in the background while your app is not running. We do not store any personal information linked to this location data.

To know which data belongs to you we use a unique app installation id. This means there is a direct link to the location data we track and your specific app installation. If you uninstall the app, this link is severed.

If you are COVID-19 positive we use your location data to inform others that have crossed paths with you and that may be at risk.

If you are not COVID-19 positive we use your data to cross reference against known at risk location points to inform you if you are at risk or not.

Your data is private, it will never be passed on to a third party. It will never be sold.

Location data may be shared with Government agencies to responsibly aid in contact tracing and preventing virus spread.

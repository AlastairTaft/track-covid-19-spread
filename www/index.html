TAS Trace Together

One of the most useful tools we have in limiting the spread of the virus is contact tracing. If we can quickly inform those who are at risk of unknowingly contracting the virus, those individuals can take precautions to prevent passing it on to others. Speed is an advantage.

What is it?

TAS Trace Together is an app built to aid community contact tracing. It was built with Tasmania in-mind as being the target audience. But there's no reason it can't be used elsewhere. Or the code can be forked and used by other developers in other regions.

Where can I get it?

Both the Apple store and Google Play store are not accepting submissions relating to COVID-19 to limit the spread of misinformation. Therefore at the moment it's only possible to download and install directly for Android, link below.

  • Android

How does it work?

It tracks your location anonymously. If you have been diagnosed with COVID-19, you can report this through the app. Anyone who has been in the same place, at the same time, while you are potentially infectious and who is also using the app will be informed. They can then take precautions to limit the spread, such as self isolating.
